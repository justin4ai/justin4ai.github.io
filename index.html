About

I'm a first-year M.S./Ph.D. integrated student at KAIST Graduate School of AI (GSAI), supervised by Prof. Jaegul Choo. My research interests include modeling lifelike avatars — particularly relightable and animatable representations — and subject-conditioned video generation.

I received my B.E. in Data Science from Hanyang University. While pursuing the degree, I worked as an AI researcher at dob Studio Inc., and as a research intern at the Samsung Electronics AI Center.
